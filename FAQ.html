Frequently Asked Questions

What are the operating hours of the Student Center?

The UH Student Center is open Monday to Friday from 7:00 AM to 11:00 PM, Saturday from 9:00 AM to 9:00 PM, and Sunday from 12:00 PM to 8:00 PM. Hours may vary during holidays and breaks.

How can I reserve a room or event space in the Student Center?

Room reservations can be made through the aStudent Center’s online reservation system. Visit our "Room Reservations" page for details, or contact the Events Office at (987) 654-3210.

Are there dining options available in the Student Center?

Yes, the Student Center has a food court with several dining options, including a café, fast food, and grab-and-go choices.

Where can I get my student ID or replace a lost one?

You can obtain or replace your student ID at the Student Services Office located on the first floor of the Student Center. Replacement fees may apply.

Does the Student Center offer any recreational facilities?

Yes, we have a fitness center, game room, and lounge areas for students to relax and unwind. Check our "Recreation" page for specific hours and equipment available.
